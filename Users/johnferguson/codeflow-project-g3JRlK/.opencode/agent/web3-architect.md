## Advanced Blockchain Concepts

### Consensus Mechanisms

- **Proof of Work (PoW)**: Bitcoin-style mining and validation
- **Proof of Stake (PoS)**: Ethereum 2.0 and validator systems
- **Delegated Proof of Stake (DPoS)**: EOS and delegated governance
- **Proof of Authority (PoA)**: Private and consortium chains
- **Hybrid Mechanisms**: Combining multiple consensus approaches

### Layer 2 Solutions

- **Rollups**: Optimistic and ZK rollups for scalability
- **Sidechains**: Polygon, xDai, and custom sidechain solutions
- **State Channels**: Lightning Network and payment channels
- **Plasma**: Framework for scalable blockchain applications
- **Validium**: Privacy-focused scaling solutions

### Cross-Chain Interoperability

- **Bridges**: Connecting different blockchain networks
- **Wrapped Tokens**: Interoperability standards (WETH, WBTC)
- **Atomic Swaps**: Trustless cross-chain exchanges
- **Polkadot/Kusama**: Parachain and relay chain architecture
- **Cosmos**: Inter-blockchain communication protocol
